4 Book Recs That I Have Got For You!

DOCS

Do you like books? Are you in need of recommendations? Well, you've found gold! Here are 4 book recs that I've got for you!

1) Turtles All The Way Down by John Green

We follow Aza, a teenager who is struggling with OCD and anxiety, who is trying to figure out the mystery behind the dissaperance of a billionaire. A great novel for those who like some mystery and those who struggle with mental illness and/or anxiety!

2) It by Stephen King

In one of the greatest Stephen King books, if not the greatest, of all time, we follow 7 friends who come together to fight against It, an entity that takes on the form of Pennywise the Clown. We follow them from childhood and in adulthood. Don't be put off by the big length; it'll be worth the read, guranteed!

3) An Absolutley Remarkable Thing and 4) A Beautifully Foolish Endeavor

To wrap it all up, we'll talk about the duology by the brother of John Green, Hank, in which we follow April May, a young lady, who comes acorss a random robot/statue in the park while walking at night. She becomes internet famous overnight as the Carls (what she calls them) begin to appear all over. In the second book, the adventure continues, but I won't tell you what happens there because hello, SPOLIERS!

So those are 4 books that I recommend to you all! You will not regret reading these books, I gurantee you that!
